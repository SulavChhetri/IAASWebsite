About Us

We are the biggest association in the world that gathers students with agricultural related fields of study.Our aim is to exchange ideas, knowledge and information among students. IAAS (International Association of Students in Agricultural and Related Sciences) is the world biggest student organization in the field of agriculture and related sciences. We are the branch of IAAS World in Asia Pasific Region with the largest number of member in the World. We have more than 1200 active members in Indonesia located in 11 Local Committees all over Indonesia. IAAS Indonesia is striving to make Agriculture flourish in the eye of young Generation in Nepal by Creating Projects, Exchange Programs, and Creative Campaigns.
